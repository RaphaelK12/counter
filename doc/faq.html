FAQ

Ther are too much files in project. Can you reduce?
No.
You can do that.

Steps are:
  1. Keep next two files: counter.hpp and counter.pp2f.hpp and delete others.
  2. In counter.hpp add line #define COUNTER_USE_PP2FILE before #if defined COUNTER_USE_PP2FILE
    or define macro COUNTER_USE_PP2FILE to be visible before include of counter.hpp.
